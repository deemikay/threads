The Thread Roll

Whenever you make a Weave: roll 2d6 + Tension

Success (9–12)

You succeed and gain a Boost.

The GM adds a Boost to a Tone Thread.

Check parity using the raw 2d6 total:

Even: Clear success

Odd: Sting in the tail → add 1 Burden to a Thread.

The GM narrates the outcome.

Twist (6–8)

You succeed but the GM introduces a Twist…

Failure (2–5)

You fail and gain a Burden.

The GM adds a Burden to a Tone Thread.

Check parity using the raw 2d6 total:

Even: Full failure

Odd: Silver lining → add 1 Boost to a Thread.

The GM introduces an Immediate Consequence!

No matter the outcome, the GM narrates the result through a Tone Thread.
